# Capture Missionaries Mod for Civilization VII

## Features
Take control of religious warfare with the ability to capture enemy missionaries during wartime!

### Core Features:
- **Capture Enemy Missionaries**: During wartime, you can now capture enemy missionaries with military units
- **Missionary Combat**: Missionaries have health that must be reduced to zero to capture them

### Balanced Gameplay Mechanics
- Missionaries can still travel to foreign territories without open borders
- Missionaries can no longer stack with other units, requiring more strategic positioning
- Each missionary occupies its own tile

### Enhanced Religious Gameplay
This mod adds strategic depth to religious competition by allowing civilizations to directly counter missionary activity through military action during wartime.

## How To Capture Missionaries
1. You must be at war with the civilization that owns the missionary
2. Attack the missionary with a military unit to reduce its health to zero
3. Once defeated, the missionary will be captured
